How it works

A matching platform with
an explicit theory underneath it.

01

Determine your type

Work through a structured onboarding questionnaire. Bring an existing type from the community if you have one — confidence scoring means we handle uncertainty honestly.

02

Choose your dynamic

Select which intertype relations you’re open to. Duals for deep complementarity. Mirrors for intellectual sparring. You set the terms — not a black-box algorithm.

03

Match with purpose

Dating at launch. Friendship, networking, and team building to follow. The same theory applies across all contexts — a Dual is a Dual whether you’re dating or building a team.

“Not compatible or incompatible — but named, characterised dynamics each with a predictable character.”

Socionics was developed in the 1970s on Jungian foundations. Unlike MBTI or the Big Five, it is primarily a theory of intertype relations — the unit of analysis is the dyad, not the person. This app is the first large-scale empirical test of those predictions.
