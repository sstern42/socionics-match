How it works

You choose the dynamic.
Not a black-box algorithm.

Work through a structured onboarding questionnaire. Bring an existing type from the community if you have one — confidence scoring means we handle uncertainty honestly.

“Unlike MBTI or the Big Five, Socionics is primarily a theory of intertype relations. The unit of analysis is the dyad, not the person.”

Developed in the 1970s on Jungian foundations, Socionics maps 16 specific dynamics between every type pair. This app is built for the English-language Socionics community — and designed to generate real data on whether the theory holds at scale.
